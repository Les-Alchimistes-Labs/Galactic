To use MAST in the Universal Render Pipeline:
  - Navigate to the FSP/MAST/Etc folder.
  - Drag Shader_Eraser_URP onto Material_Eraser
  - Drag Shader_Grid_URP onto Material_Grid
  - Drag Shader_PaintArea_URP onto Material_PaintArea
